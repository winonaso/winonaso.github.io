LRC Lyrics

Sony Entertainment Network

At Sony, I was the main designer for a major feature in Media Go, the LRC lyrics. This feature allowed users to add LRC lyrics to their music files. LRC lyrics are lyrics that have been timed to the music (for example, like karaoke).

The project consisted of two major parts; the creation of the LRC lyrics, and the playback of LRC lyrics. The project went through many iterations but I'm proud to have completed it for the major release with the feature implemented.

Lead Me! A.R. Application

Course Work

This was an augmented reality application that I helped design for a user interface and user experience course at the University of Waterloo. Three-people teams created applications that used augmented reality functionality somehow. The application was a map application that helped students (particularly first year students) navigate around the campus in order to find different buildings, rooms, and services.

The design of this application spanned over four months, and we started at the very beginning by creating a persona and workflows. Part of the course included making paper prototypes and doing real user tests. The end result is a fully designed application, with real-world user tests and paper prototypes.

Luddite Lights

Coursework

In my upper year design course, we had to emulate a course reading by hacking a DIY project and an arduino. The design project started with choosing a course reading we wanted to cover - in our case, it was Steven Jones' Against Technology.

We soldered buttons to wires, figured out how to attach all of the lights and buttons, attached them all to the arduino, and uploaded our code to the arduino.

To see our entire design process from beginning to end, check out my course blog, Our Leaderless Movement.
