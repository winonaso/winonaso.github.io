Combine & Trim Feature

Sony Entertainment Network

At Sony Entertainment Network, I was the main UI/UX designer for the major 2.5 release working on the Trim & Combine video feature. This feature allowed users to trim and combine videos. We wanted to allow the users to view their work as they go, hence the preview window and the ability to move clips around.

This wizard allowed users to trim their video clips by using the green and red sliders. The play button allowed users to play exactly from the spot that they wanted to trim the clip.
