Luddite Lites
Undergraduate Course Work

In an undergraduate design course, we explained a course reading by hacking an existing DIY project and an Arduino. We chose Steven Jones' Against Technology, and gamified the critical issues of the myth of the Luddites and the value of manual versus mechanized labour.

My partner and I worked together to solder wires, attach the LED matrix and buttons to the Arduino, and write and upload code to the Arduino.
