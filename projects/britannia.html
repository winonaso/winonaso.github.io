2010 Yearbook
Britannia Secondary
School Yearbook
Editor-in-Chief

As the editor-in-chief, I was put in charge of a team of 10 students, responsible for the creation and distribution of the annual school yearbook. This included design and layout, organization of grade photos, colecting writeups, as well as the advertising and sale of the yearbooks.

The 2010 Britannia Yearbook was the first yearbook in the school's 100th anniversary to ever sell out due to new marketing strategies and student involvement that my team and I implemented during the production of the yearbook.
