Misprint

Imprint mini-newspaper

This is a special, once a year April-fools mini edition of Imprint known as Misprint. You can also take a look at the web version here.

I was the editor of the 2015 edition. This meant I was involved in all aspects of the production of the 8-page paper. This varied from finding writers, photographers, graphic designers, as well as writing stories and laying out the paper.
