Undergraduate Course Work
Designer

This was an augmented reality application that I design for a UI/UX course at the University of Waterloo. Three-people teams created applications that utilized augmented reality. It aided students navigate around the campus in order to find buildings, rooms, and services.

The design of this application spanned over four months. The course included creating personas, workflows, paper prototypes as well as face-to-face user tests.
