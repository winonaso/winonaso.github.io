Book Cover

Project

This was a book cover created for Walter Benjamin's The Work of Art in the Age of Mechanical Reproduction. The concept was that the statue of the historical, Egyptian cat was slowly transformed into a mechanical reproduction of the cat.

Shots - a Film

Course Work

This project was a huge 4 month project, where a group of us came together to write, draw storyboards, film, and edit a short film. We decided to base our story around a photographer who goes about his day, taking photos of people. At the end of the day, the audience realizes that things are not always as they seem.

Thanks to Ralon Nazareth, Mariah Perry, Valerie Petersen, and Michael To for creating this film with me.

An extremely greatful thanks to the Canadian Centre of Arts and Technology for providing the equiptment to film with.
