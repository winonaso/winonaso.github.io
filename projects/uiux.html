LRC Lyrics Feature

Sony Entertainment Network
(Co-op term)

During my terms at Sony Entertainment Network, I was the main UI/UX designer for one of the major 2.5 releases, the LRC lyrics feature. This feature allowed users to add LRC lyrics to their music files. This was the first major UI/UX project that had a huge potential userbase. LRC lyrics are lyrics that have been timed to the music (for example, like karaoke).

The project consisted of two major parts; the creation of the LRC lyrics, and the playback of LRC lyrics. The project went through many, many iterations but I'm proud to have completed it for the major release.

To see it in action, download Media Go 2.5.

To read more about my workterm at Sony, check out my work experience page.

Lead Me! A.R. Application

Course Work

This was an augmented reality application that I helped design for a user interface and user experience course at the University of Waterloo. The teams of 3 people had to create an application that used augmented reality functionality somehow. The application was a map application that helped students (particularly first year students) navigate around the campus in order to find different buildings, rooms, and services.

The design of this application spanned over four months, and we started at the very beginning by creating a persona and workflows. Part of the course included making paper prototypes and doing real user tests. The end result is a fully designed application, with real-world user tests and paper prototypes.
