John Orpheus Booklet

This was a booklet created to promote the indie film, John Orpheus Must Die. It was designed with the elements of the film in mind, and tried to emulate the ideas and colours of the film. The main illustration was drawn by myself, and can be seen in the following project. I drew the image of John Orpheus using vectors in Adobe Illustrator.
