LRC Lyrics

Sony Entertainment Network

At Sony, I was the main designer for a major feature in Media Go, the LRC lyrics. This feature allowed users to add LRC lyrics to their music files. LRC lyrics are lyrics that have been timed to the music (for example, like karaoke).

The project consisted of two major parts; the creation of the LRC lyrics, and the playback of LRC lyrics. The project went through many iterations but I'm proud to have completed it for the major release with the feature implemented.
