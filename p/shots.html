Shots - a Film

During one of my Digital Arts Communication classes at the University of Waterloo, I, along with a group of other individuals came together to write, draw storyboards, film, and edit a short film. We decided to base our story around a photographer who goes about his day, taking photos of people. At the end of the day, the audience realizes that things are not always as they seem.

Thanks to Ralon Nazareth, Mariah Perry, Valerie Petersen, and Michael To, for creating this film with me. This film wouldn't have been made possible without the Canadian Centre of Arts and Technology for providing the equiptment to film with.

After many hours of filming, and even more hours of editing, we are proud to present, Shots.
