about me

Hi! I’m a recent University of Waterloo English and Business graduate, specializing in Digital Arts Communication. I am highly motivated, and passionate about what I do.

My tools of the trade are Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Indesign. I also have experience working with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Captivate, Adobe Audition, as well as HTML and CSS.

Although I primarily love working on front end design, I also have experience in wireframe and workflow prototyping, copywriting, social media presence, and video production as well.

To check out my most recent projects, check out my blog.
