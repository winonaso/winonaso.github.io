about me

I am a freelance board game designer, developer, speaker, and consultant.

I have experience working with a variety of license intellectual properties (Back to the Future, TMNT, Nickelodeon, etc), and creating original IP.

GAME CREDITS

With over 45 games signed, and experience working with 15 publishers, I love collaborating with a variety of designers and industry experts.

SERVICES

A few services I have provided include: game invention (both creating original material and license games), game development, playtesting, project management, designer relations, publisher relations, international localization, crowd funding support, and overall brand management.

Additionally, I enjoy speaking at conventions and on a variety of podcasts.
