PARTICIPANTS NEEDED FOR RESEARCH IN
SNAPCHAT, BITMOJI, AND THEME PARKS

Ever waited in line?
Used Snapchat or Bitmoji?
Been to a theme park?
Used a Disney MagicBand?

If you answered yes to any of above questions you are invited to volunteer in this study of understanding the motivations of people who use Bitmoji, and what people do while they wait in line in order to design an avatar system for the Theme Park Industry.

You will be asked to fill in an anonymous online survey.

Your participation will involve a single online session for about 20 minutes.

You will not be paid for taking part in this study.

If you are interested in participating in this study or for more information please contact:
